Script Writing

Script writing is the process of creating dialogues, scenes, and storylines for films, TV shows, or plays. It requires creativity, structure, and a strong understanding of storytelling. A good script includes engaging characters, clear conflicts, and a well-paced plot. Writers often revise their scripts multiple times to improve dialogue and narrative flow. shaping how stories are told on screen and stage.
